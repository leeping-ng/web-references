Bugatti Chiron/ LEGO Technic #42083

The Bugatti Chiron is a mid-engine two-seater sports car designed and developed in Germany by Bugatti Engineering GmbH. The successor to the Bugatti Veyron, the Chiron was first shown at the Geneva Motor Show on 1 March 2016.

This was released as a LEGO set #42083 in 2018, featuring a working 8-speed paddle shift transmission, W16 engine with moving pistons, front and rear suspension, and 4-wheel drive.
